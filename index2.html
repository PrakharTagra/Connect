What is Connect?

A platform that bridges the gap between students and alumni.
Built to support mentorship, opportunities, and meaningful conversations.
Helps students find guidance, internships, and career inspiration.
Empowers alumni to reconnect, share experiences, and discover talent.
Ensures that every story and skill is heard, shared, and valued.
Fosters a network where growth, support, and giving go hand in hand.
